Angularists Unite!

We are very excited & eager to be an official remote extension of the highly anticpated AngularJS conference ng-conf, taking place March 5th & 6th in Salt Lake City, Utah.

Although we are not capable of teleporting everyone who RSVP's to Salt Lake City for the conference, we are able to live stream the entire ng-conf onto our giant projector screens, geek-out on Angular swag, hack on cool projects with other Target Devs, & learn how many of these technologies are being used by talented Developers around Target Engineering by simply getting together.
